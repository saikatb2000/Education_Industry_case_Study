A prominent name in the Digital Journey: Data-Driven Success for a New Educational Institutio

In January, a strategic partnership was formed with a newly established college to enhance its online presence and drive audience engagement. The goal was to target three audience segments: Primary (prospective students and direct stakeholders), Secondary (alumni, parents, and students in early consideration stages), and Tertiary (general viewers). The Primary Audience emerged as the most engaged, achieving over 20 million interactions, demonstrating strong alignment with the college's messaging. The Secondary Audience achieved 2 million interactions, reflecting consistent retention. Although the Tertiary Audience had minimal engagement, it contributed to overall visibility.


By tailoring content to resonate with each group, retention improved significantly, especially between June and September. This multi-pronged approach positioned the college to compete effectively with established institutions, laying the groundwork for sustained growth and enhanced brand recognition in the education sector.


Targeted Content Strategies on Facebook

A demographic analysis of Facebook engagement highlighted a significant concentration of users in the 18-24 and 25-34 age groups, aligning perfectly with the college's target audience. Minimal engagement from older age brackets, such as 55+, confirmed the effectiveness of this focused strategy. By concentrating on younger demographics, the campaigns maximized relevance and ensured resource efficiency.

The data also revealed a distinct preference for video content, which accounted for the highest engagement levels, surpassing 3.3 million interactions. This was followed by links (2.7 million interactions) and photos (1.7 million interactions). Recognizing this trend, the strategy shifted toward producing dynamic and visually engaging video posts, leading to improved interaction rates and stronger connections with prospective students.

Geographical Insights and Engagement Patterns

Analyzing views by city revealed Kolkata as the top-performing region, with the highest percentage of audience engagement. Other key cities, including Delhi, Gauhati, Faridabad, Patna, Asansol, and Dhanbad, also demonstrated notable viewership, reflecting broad geographical reach across metro and Tier-II cities. These insights are instrumental in tailoring region-specific campaigns to maximize reach and impact.

Performance of Google Ads Campaigns

The branding campaign on Google Ads was a pivotal initiative, focusing on Demand Generation and Display Ads. This effort aimed to boost visibility among prospective students by leveraging a high-impression strategy. The campaigns generated significant impressions and interaction rates, marking an impactful start in building awareness and a strong digital presence.
